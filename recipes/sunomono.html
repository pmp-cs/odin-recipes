Sunomono(Japanese Cucumber Salad)

Sunomono is a Japanese cuisine using cucumber. It is a sweet and sour salad and usually used as a side dish. It is delicous and very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Slice crosswise into very thin slices.
  2. Sprinkle salt on sliced cucumbers and wait 5 minutes.
  3. Discard the water that comes out while lightly pressing down on the cucumber.
  4. Mix rice vinegar, sugar and soy sauce together.
  5. Add cucumber slices and stir to coat.
